Welcome to I am an AIESECer



I am an AIESECer is a networking platform for AIESEC members in Facebook.



This application aims to provide a platform enabling AIESECers to connect in a virtual structured world. The main purpose of this application is to create AIESECer identity for you in facebook and enable you to

  • Connect to your teammates
  • Record your AIESEC experience
  • Provide an easy access to your profile, files, and wikis in MyAIESEC.net
